
# AthenaAI StudyBuddy - Application Features

Here is a summary of the features in the AthenaAI StudyBuddy application, perfect for your presentation.

---

## 1. Dashboard

**The central hub of the application.**

- **Welcome Message:** Greets the user with a personalized message.
- **Feature Navigation:** Displays a grid of beautifully designed cards, each representing a core feature of the app. This provides quick, one-click access to all tools.
- **Inspirational Hero Image:** Showcases a large, motivational image related to studying to inspire users as soon as they log in.

---

## 2. Quiz Generator

**Test your knowledge with AI-powered quizzes.**

- **Generate from Topic:** Users can simply enter a topic (e.g., "Photosynthesis"), and the AI will create a multiple-choice quiz.
- **Generate from PDF:** Users can upload their own study materials (lecture notes, textbooks) as a PDF, and the AI will generate a relevant quiz based on the document's content.
- **Interactive Quiz Experience:** Users can take the quiz directly in the app.
- **Instant Feedback & AI Explanations:** After submitting, the app checks the answers and provides immediate feedback. For incorrect answers, the AI explains *why* the answer was wrong and clarifies the correct concept.
- **Downloadable Results:** Users can download a PDF report of their quiz results, complete with questions, answers, and AI explanations.

---

## 3. AI Summarizer

**Condense long texts into key points.**

- **Summarize from Text:** Paste any long article or text into a text area for an instant summary.
- **Summarize from PDF:** Upload a dense research paper or textbook chapter, and the AI will provide a concise summary.
- **Focused Summarization:** Users can guide the AI to focus on a specific topic or page range within an uploaded PDF for more targeted summaries.
- **Downloadable Summary:** The generated summary can be downloaded as a cleanly formatted PDF.

---

## 4. Paper Generator

**Your personal assistant for creating exam and practice papers.**

- **Custom Paper Creation:** Users can specify a topic, the number of multiple-choice, short-answer, and long-answer questions to generate a custom exam paper.
- **AI-Powered Remedial Papers:** This intelligent feature analyzes a user's entire quiz history, identifies their weakest subjects, and automatically generates a "remedial" practice paper that specifically targets those areas for improvement.
- **Interactive Paper-Taking:** Users can fill out their answers to the generated paper directly within the app.
- **AI-Grading and Feedback:** The AI checks the user's answers against the correct ones and provides explanations for mistakes.
- **Downloadable Marked Paper:** The final, graded paper with scores and feedback can be downloaded as a professional-looking PDF report.

---

## 5. Study Planner

**Organize your learning journey effectively.**

- **AI-Generated Schedules:** The AI creates a detailed, actionable study plan based on a topic and a given timeframe (e.g., "Learn Calculus in 3 weeks").
- **Context-Aware Planning:** The AI can take into account additional user specifications, such as "only study on weekends" or "focus on practical examples."
- **PDF-Based Planning:** Users can upload a syllabus or textbook, and the AI will structure the study plan around its chapters and content.
- **Downloadable Plan:** The structured, session-by-session plan can be downloaded as a PDF table.

---

## 6. Study Timer (Pomodoro)

**Stay focused and avoid burnout.**

- **Classic Pomodoro Technique:** Implements the proven method of focused study sessions (e.g., 25 minutes) followed by short and long breaks.
- **Customizable Timers:** Users can configure the length of study sessions, short breaks, and long breaks to fit their personal workflow.
- **Session Tracking:** The timer keeps track of completed study sessions and cycles.
- **Audio & Visual Alerts:** Provides notifications when it's time to switch between studying and breaking.
- **Automatic Logging:** Completed study sessions are automatically saved to the user's progress tracker.

---

## 7. Image to Text (OCR & Explanation)

**Digitize and understand your physical notes.**

- **Extract Text:** Users can upload an image of handwritten notes, a textbook page, or a diagram. The AI performs Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to extract all the text.
- **Content-Aware Analysis:** If the image contains no text (like a diagram), the AI provides a detailed description of the visual content.
- **AI Explanation:** With one click, users can ask the AI to generate a detailed explanation of the extracted text or the image's content.
- **Key Term Explanations:** The AI can identify and provide definitions for key terms and concepts found in the text.
- **Downloadable Content:** All extracted and generated content (text, explanations, terms) can be downloaded as PDFs.

---

## 8. Progress Tracker

**Visualize your learning and growth.**

- **At-a-Glance Stats:** Key metrics like total study time, average quiz score, and number of "mastered" subjects are displayed prominently.
- **Quiz Performance Chart:** A bar chart shows the user's average quiz score for each subject, making it easy to identify strengths and weaknesses.
- **Study Time Chart:** Another chart visualizes the total time spent studying for each subject.
- **Downloadable Report:** Users can download a comprehensive progress report in PDF format, including all their stats and charts.

---

## 9. User Settings

**Personalize your AthenaAI experience.**

- **Profile Management:** Users can update their username and college name.
- **Subject Management:** Users can add or remove the subjects they are currently studying, which customizes the options available throughout the app.
