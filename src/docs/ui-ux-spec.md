
# StudyMate UI/UX Design Specification

This document outlines the UI/UX design standards for the StudyMate platform, covering three distinct visual themes: "Scholarly White," "Cosmic Intellect," and "Cyberpunk Sage."

---

## 1. Dashboard

| Feature | Light Theme: "Scholarly White" | Dark Theme: "Cosmic Intellect" | Neon Theme: "Cyberpunk Sage" |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Welcome** | Simple, elegant greeting in a classic font. The message is clear and professional against a bright background. | Welcome text glows subtly, providing a sophisticated sci-fi feel. | Welcome message appears in a dynamic, fluorescent font, potentially with a slight "glitch" animation. |
| **Card Grid** | Cards have clean, off-white backgrounds with subtle, soft gray drop shadows, making them feel like physical index cards or notebooks. | Frosted glass cards allow the rich, dark blue background to show through, creating depth and separating elements with a soft glow. | Cards have transparent or dark backings with vivid, glowing, and animated borders that respond to hover actions. |
| **Quick Access** | Icons are clean line art in a deep navy, ensuring they are easily legible and non-distracting. | Icons are light, thin-lined, and stand out against the dark cards, with a subtle internal glow effect. | Icons are bright, glowing vector outlines in accent colors like cyan or magenta. |

## 2. AI Chat

| Feature | Light Theme: "Scholarly White" | Dark Theme: "Cosmic Intellect" | Neon Theme: "Cyberpunk Sage" |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chat Interface** | Clean white message bubbles for the user and soft gray bubbles for the AI, ensuring readability. Avatars are simple profile images. | User's message bubbles are light gray, and AI's are a dark, rich blue. The chat interface is high-contrast and easy on the eyes for late-night studying. | Chat bubbles are transparent or dark, and text is in vivid, glowing neon colors. AI avatars are abstract, animated shapes. |

## 3. Quiz & Paper Generators

| Feature | Light Theme: "Scholarly White" | Dark Theme: "Cosmic Intellect" | Neon Theme: "Cyberpunk Sage" |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Quiz Interface** | The quiz is presented with a clean, classic feel. Correct answers get a soft green highlight; incorrect answers get a gentle red. | An immersive, full-screen quiz experience. Correct answers are highlighted with a cool green glow; incorrect answers get a subtle red overlay. | The interface is minimalist, with stark black backgrounds and vibrant neon text. Correct answers flash with a satisfying green spark; incorrect answers flicker red. |
| **Generator Process** | A professional loading screen shows the AI "writing" the paper with a clean progress bar. | An animation of data flowing in a network shows the AI analyzing exam papers. The progress bar fills with a smooth, glowing animation. | The loading screen features a fast-paced, circuit-like animation, showcasing the high-tech, predictive nature of the tool. |

## 4. Study Tools (Summarizer, Notes, OCR)

| Feature | Light Theme: "Scholarly White" | Dark Theme: "Cosmic Intellect" | Neon Theme: "Cyberpunk Sage" |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **AI Summarizer** | The summarized text is presented in a clean, scrollable panel, with key terms subtly bolded for quick scanning. | The summarized text appears in a sleek, minimalist overlay. Key terms are a pop of accent color to guide the eye. | Summaries are displayed as glowing text blocks. The interface has pulsing, animated icons to indicate a document is being processed. |
| **Notes Maker** | The generated notes appear in a formatted document-like view, with clear headings and bullet points, mimicking a real textbook. | The notes appear in a sophisticated, distraction-free interface. Headers have a soft, colorful underline, and key terms are subtly tinted. | Notes are formatted with neon-colored headings and lists. The interface includes animated brackets and cursor effects to suggest constant AI presence. |
| **Image to Text (OCR)** | A crisp interface shows the uploaded image next to the extracted text. The text extraction animation is a clean, quick visual of the image being "scanned". | The image is displayed dimly on one side, and the extracted text appears brightly on the other, creating a sense of focus and clarity. | The image appears to be quickly digitized with a bright, grid-like scanning animation before the extracted text appears in a vibrant font. |

## 5. Study Timer & Progress Tracker

| Feature | Light Theme: "Scholarly White" | Dark Theme: "Cosmic Intellect" | Neon Theme: "Cyberpunk Sage" |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Study Timer** | The Pomodoro timer is a classic, readable interface with clean lines and clear text. It is reminiscent of a physical clock face. | The timer features a subtle, circular glow that recedes as time passes, creating a calming, immersive experience. | The timer is a digital-style display with large, fluorescent numbers. Time-based notifications flash with a bright, energetic light. |
| **Progress Tracker** | Performance data is shown in crisp, easy-to-read bar and line charts. Ample white space prevents the data from feeling overwhelming. | Interactive data visualizations with elegant lines and graphs appear against a dark background. Stats and charts feel sophisticated and focused. | Progress charts use glowing neon lines and bars that animate dynamically. The display is reminiscent of an AI processing unit. |

## 6. History & User Settings

| Feature | Light Theme: "Scholarly White" | Dark Theme: "Cosmic Intellect" | Neon Theme: "Cyberpunk Sage" |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **History** | The history is presented as a clean, chronological list of entries, easy to scan and review. | The history log uses a minimalist, console-like interface, where entries are displayed with a consistent, readable format. | The history shows a dynamic, glowing log of activity, with past entries appearing in a scrolling, digital format. |
| **User Settings** | A classic settings interface with a clear, categorized menu. | Settings are presented on translucent cards with minimalist icons. | The UI is sleek and futuristic, with bright toggles and sliders that stand out. |
