# AthenaAI StudyBuddy

For **setup, environment variables, full feature documentation, and Connectors hub behavior**, see the repository root [README.md](../README.md).

Welcome to **AthenaAI StudyBuddy**, your personal AI-powered academic assistant designed to help you excel in your studies. This application leverages cutting-edge AI to provide a suite of tools that streamline learning, organization, and knowledge testing.

---

## Core Features (Common Overview)

Here is a summary of the main features available in the application:

- **Dashboard**: A central hub that provides a personalized welcome and quick access to all features.
- **AI Chat**: An intelligent, context-aware chatbot that acts as a personal academic advisor, leveraging your study data to provide tailored advice.
- **Quiz Generator**: Create custom multiple-choice quizzes by providing a topic or uploading your own PDF study materials. Get instant feedback with AI-powered explanations for incorrect answers.
- **AI Summarizer**: Condense long documents, articles, or pasted text into concise, easy-to-digest summaries. You can also summarize specific chapters or page ranges from an uploaded PDF.
- **Paper Generator**: Generate custom practice exams with a mix of multiple-choice, short-answer, and long-answer questions. It also features an AI remedial tool that creates papers based on your weakest subjects.
- **Study Planner**: Automatically generate structured, actionable study plans based on a topic, timeframe, or even the contents of a syllabus PDF.
- **Study Timer**: A Pomodoro timer to help you manage focused study sessions and breaks, with automatic logging of your progress.
- **Image to Text**: Upload images of handwritten notes or book pages to extract the text using OCR. The AI can then explain the extracted content or define key terms.
- **Progress Tracker**: Visualize your academic growth with charts and statistics on your quiz performance and study habits across all subjects.
- **History**: A comprehensive log of all your past quizzes, papers, study sessions, and summaries, with options to download or revisit them.
- **User Settings**: Manage your profile details, customize subjects, change the application theme, and handle account settings.
